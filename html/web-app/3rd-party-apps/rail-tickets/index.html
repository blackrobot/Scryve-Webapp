Welcome to the Masabi ticket purchase demo.

This application allows you to buy and store your train tickets.

This application downloads mobile tickets and stores them, so that you can quickly access them during your journey.

Select “Buy Tickets” to start the purchase process.

Once a ticket is downloaded, it will be available on your phone until the day after it expires, based on your phone’s clock and date settings.
